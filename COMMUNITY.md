# 🚀 Explore the Helwan Linux Ecosystem: Community, Coverage & Contributions

Thank you for your interest in **Helwan Linux** — an Arch-based distribution tailored for Arabic users and developers.

Helwan Linux blends the power and flexibility of Arch with out-of-the-box support for Arabic, a Cinnamon desktop experience, developer tools, and system recovery utilities. It is designed for people who want **performance, simplicity, and community-driven growth** — straight from Egypt to the world.
